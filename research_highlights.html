Research Focus Areas

Global Marine Plankton Diversity

Investigating patterns of plankton diversity and their implications for ocean ecosystems using global datasets.

Microbial Ecology

Studying the role of microbial communities in marine environments, especially in relation to host organisms and coral reefs.

Species Distribution Models (SDMs)

Developing and applying models to map species distributions in undersampled marine regions.

Biogeochemical Cycles

Examining how biodiversity contributes to key processes in global biogeochemical cycles, including carbon and nutrient flows.

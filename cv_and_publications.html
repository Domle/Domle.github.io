Professional Experience

2026 - Present
Postdoc

ETH Zürich, Switzerland

Research focuses on mapping and predicting the contemporary and future global distribution of marine plankton organisms. By integrating high-resolution microscopy with advanced metagenomic survey data, the project provides a comprehensive, multi-layered assessment of marine planktonic diversity patterns across the global ocean to offer insights into shifting microbial ecosystems and biogeochemical cycles.

2020 - 2021
Research Assistant

ETH Zürich, Switzerland

Participated in the Geneva Science-Policy Interface project MAPMAKER, projecting future plankton distributions using climate models and Representative Concentration Pathways. Developed an interactive map to guide international policy decisions on marine biodiversity protection. | mapmaker-new.ethz.ch
